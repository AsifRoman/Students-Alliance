About Us

Hello Everyone, Kamarhati Students' Group being a voice of students of kamarhati working on various initiatives to protect the rights of the students in all level, and on developing of Education system in Kamarhati, and many other problems regarding Education, Social and Economical.

OUR PROJECT

Rally againt NRC/CAA

Date : 21st December, 2019

We, the students of Kamarhati, organised a non-political, non-religion, and non-violent protest against the unconstitutional law CAA and NRC.

Almost all the students and teachers of Kamarhati attended this rally. A big thank you to our Kamarhati OutPost Officers for giving permission and protecting from all riots.

Door to door Stickers Campaign

Date : 31st January, 2020

We, the students of Kamarhati, started a door to door campaign against the unconstitutional law CAA and NRC. In this campaign, we stick the Stickers of "Hum Kaagaz Nhi Dhikhayege, Hum Kagaz Nhi Likhayege, Ha par Chhai zarur Pilaayege".

Approx 5000 stickers are printed in almost half of the localities of Kamarhati.

Lockdown Relief: For You , With You

Date : 25th March, 2020

During pandemic Kamarhati students group have been reached more than 800 families covering the area of Kumarpara, Craig road, Chingri talab, Nil Ratan Adhikari(NRI) road, Phool bagan , New Basti, Balu talab, Dhobia Bagan, Dasu bagan, Anwar Bagan, Bans bagan , Kathal Bagan , Phandi bagan etc. Almost every corner of kamarhati.

We helped almost 800-850 peoples in this pandemic.

Midday-meal Program

Date : 30th April, 2020

In second phase of Lockdown with the help of Apni Roti, we have reached 500-600 families relief daily with midday-meal (khichdi) at afternoon lunch time for a week.

We served 500-600 families daily for a week in Kumarpara, New Basti, BT Road Basti and Israil Coloni.

Eid kit Program

Publish Date : 23th May, 2020

On the eve of Eid Mubarak as on 25th of May 2020, We had a plan for Eid Kits and on 23rd May 2020 140 families got Eid kits from Kamarhati Students group.

Before Eid, we distributed rations for Eid and Sewai as a Eidi.

Khichda Distribution on Eid

Publish Date : 25th May, 2020

On the eve of Eid Mubarak as on 25th of May 2020, Kamarhati Students Group distributed cooked food for beggers, Street families.The whole program runs under the banner of kamarhati Students Group in support of different organisations and individual donors we really glad to have them.

We served Khichda for 250 peoples on the auspicious day of Eid.
